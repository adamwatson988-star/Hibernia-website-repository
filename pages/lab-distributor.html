How to Apply

To apply for a Lab or Distributor account:

  1. Complete our standard research access application
  2. Indicate your account type (Laboratory or Distributor)
  3. Provide organization details and relevant credentials
  4. Our team will review your application
  5. Upon approval, receive your professional account access
