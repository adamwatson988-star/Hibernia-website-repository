HRC Research Support Hub

Welcome to the Hibernia Research Compounds Support Hub. Find answers to common questions, access resources, and get help with your research needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for research access?

Visit our Apply for Research Access page and complete the application form. Our team will review your application within 2-3 business days. Upon approval, you'll receive access to our full product catalog.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods through our secure payment processing system. Payment links are issued manually after your order is finalized. Contact our support team for specific payment inquiries.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary based on destination and order complexity. Standard processing typically takes 3-5 business days after payment. International orders may require additional time for customs clearance.

Do you provide bulk pricing?

Yes! Bulk pricing is available for large orders. For details on volume discounts, please apply for a professional account through our Lab & Distributor Access program or contact our sales team.

Can I get product documentation and certificates?

Yes. All products include independent analytical testing reports, purity reviews, and complete documentation. Visit our Product Documentation page for more information.

What is the return policy?

All sales are final for research compounds. However, if there are any issues with your order (damage, incorrect items), please contact us immediately with details and photographic evidence for resolution options.
